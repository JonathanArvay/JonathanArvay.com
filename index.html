Current work

I am validating a deterministic analysis suite intended to support tax controversy, audit support, and compliance operations teams. The goal is to standardize factual analysis steps and produce artifacts suitable for review and escalation.

Capabilities (non-advisory)

  • Document completeness analysis and dependency detection
  • Structured classification with evidence anchors
  • Filing category routing based on explicit indicators
  • IRS notice content extraction (deadlines, required documents, procedural text)

Outputs are designed to be deterministic and reviewable. Ambiguity is surfaced explicitly rather than resolved by inference.

Boundary statement

The systems described here are intentionally constrained.

  • No tax advice, legal advice, financial recommendations, or compliance determinations
  • No automated decisions or “best action” suggestions
  • No hidden inference: unknowns remain unknown and are flagged explicitly
  • Evidence anchoring is required for classifications and findings
